📬 Capital Referrals Sent

Import the "Capital Referrals Sent Service" Salesforce report. All lending partner types (Epos Now Capital, YouLend, etc.) are combined into one referral count per person. Used to calculate conversion rates alongside Capital conversions.

📬
Drop Referrals Sent Report Here

or click to browse

Capital Referrals Sent Service - KS .xlsx

💡 How conversion rate is calculated:
Rate % = Capital Conversions ÷ Referrals Sent × 100

On the Leaderboard, switching to Capital shows everyone who sent referrals — greyed out until they convert. Converters float to the top with their rate shown.

On the Report page, a funnel chart appears above the Capital section showing sent vs converted over time.
